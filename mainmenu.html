Our Story

Welcome to ZORKO

Founded in 2015, ZORKO began as a small food stall with a big dream - to serve delicious, affordable food that brings people together. What started as a humble venture has now blossomed into one of the most beloved eateries in town, known for our innovative flavors and commitment to quality.

Our Philosophy

At ZORKO, we believe food should be three things: flavorful, fresh, and fun. We source local ingredients whenever possible and prepare everything with care and attention to detail. Our menu blends traditional recipes with modern twists, creating unique taste experiences you won't find anywhere else.

Quality Ingredients

We use only the freshest vegetables, premium cheeses, and authentic spices in all our dishes.

Innovative Recipes

Our chefs constantly experiment to bring you exciting new flavor combinations.

Customer Happiness

Your satisfaction is our top priority - we aim to delight with every bite.

Meet Our Team

The heart of ZORKO is our passionate team of chefs, servers, and managers who work tirelessly to create memorable dining experiences. Led by Head Chef Rajesh Kumar, our culinary team brings together decades of combined experience in Indian and international cuisines.

Come taste the ZORKO difference today!
